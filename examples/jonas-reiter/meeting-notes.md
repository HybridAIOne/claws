# Revenue Ops Sync

Date: March 18, 2026

Attendees:
- Jonas Reiter, Meeting Intelligence Coordinator
- Maya Stein, Product Manager
- Daniel Cho, Sales Operations Lead
- Priya Nair, Finance Partner
- Luca Vogel, Customer Success Manager

Agenda:
- Decide when billing holds should block workspace exports
- Clarify who owns enterprise exception approvals
- Agree on a shared tracker for follow-ups and due dates
- Prepare a recommendation for the weekly leadership review

Notes:
- Priya said finance wants export blocking only for hard holds, not for short-term payment delays or invoices that are already being resolved.
- Daniel said sales needs one named owner for enterprise exceptions so customers do not get routed through three different people.
- Luca asked for a short customer-facing explanation that support can reuse without rewriting it for every account.
- Maya said the product team can support a softer path for edge cases, but only if approvals are visible in one place.
- Everyone agreed the current mix of Slack threads and personal to-do lists is not working.

Decisions:
- Hard billing holds will block workspace exports.
- Soft holds will not block exports if a manager approves the exception.
- Daniel Cho will own the exception escalation process for enterprise accounts.
- The team will use one shared tracker with owners, due dates, and approval status.

Open Questions:
- What is the exact cutoff for a "hard" hold?
- Should renewals inside the final 48 hours get an automatic exception review?
- Who signs off when Sales and Finance disagree on a borderline account?

Action Items:
- Maya Stein will draft the policy language and circulate it for review by March 20.
- Daniel Cho will define the escalation path and add named backups by March 21.
- Priya Nair will list the billing scenarios that should always block exports by March 21.
- Luca Vogel will write the customer-facing explanation and support macro by March 22.
- Jonas Reiter will turn this note into a leadership recap and post the tracker link in the team channel by end of day.
