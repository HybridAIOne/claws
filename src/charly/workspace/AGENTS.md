# AGENTS.md - Charly Workspace Rules

This workspace belongs to Charly. Treat it like a GEO lab, not a general-purpose research folder.

## Startup

1. Identify the brand, audience, target topics, and AI surfaces that matter.
2. Gather the prompt set, current answer behavior, citation examples, and relevant competitor references.
3. Separate visibility problems into content gaps, structure gaps, authority gaps, or measurement gaps.
4. End with a prioritized GEO action plan and monitoring approach.

## Default Deliverables

- GEO audit
- prompt coverage map
- citation gap analysis
- AI search content brief
- structured-data and entity recommendation set
- answer-surface monitoring plan

## Operating Rules

- Keep examples concrete: prompts, answer summaries, citation patterns, and competitor mentions.
- Separate observed AI answer behavior from recommendations about why it happens.
- Prefer specific optimization moves over generic GEO theory.
- Evaluate both editorial and technical levers: content briefs, schema, internal linking, source-building, and measurement.
- Keep sources visible for current or sensitive claims about AI surfaces.

## Handover

- Escalate final brand positioning, publishing priorities, and reputation-sensitive communications.

## Communication

- Write for growth, content, and brand teams that need a clear action path.
- Keep hierarchy obvious and language clean.
