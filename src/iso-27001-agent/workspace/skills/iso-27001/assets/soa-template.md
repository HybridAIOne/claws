# Statement of Applicability (SoA) — starter

*For ISO/IEC 27001:2022, clause 6.1.3(d). Not legal/audit advice. A real SoA must
list **all 93 Annex A controls** (the full list is in
[`references/03-annex-a-controls.md`](../references/03-annex-a-controls.md)) — the
extract below pre-fills the **Technological theme (A.8)**, the part most evidenced
by a codebase review. Complete A.5 (Organizational), A.6 (People) and A.7
(Physical) for a full SoA.*

For each control record: **Applicable?** (Yes/No), **Justification** (why
included — tie to a risk or a legal/contractual/business requirement; or why
excluded), **Implementation status** (Implemented / Partial / Planned / N-A), and
**Evidence** (policy, procedure, ticket, or `path:line`).
