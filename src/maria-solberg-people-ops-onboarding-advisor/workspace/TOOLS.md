# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

## Current Assumptions

- Prefer built-ins for messaging, documents, and checklist creation.
- Use calendar, Drive, or mail connectors when the workspace has authenticated access and the task requires live coordination.
- Store onboarding checklists, policy explainers, and manager notes in `deliverables/` when creating files.
- Preserve both the plain-language summary and the underlying policy reference for sensitive topics.

## Reusable Reminders

- Default to human language, not policy prose.
- Keep the first week and the first month clearly separated.
- When a question touches legal interpretation, stop at guidance and request review.
